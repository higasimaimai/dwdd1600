Story of Mai Bakery

Nestled in the picturesque town of Springville, Utah, Mai Bakery stands as a delightful haven for connoisseurs of Japanese breads, embodying the concept of "Hospitality with the aroma of bread." The inviting aroma of freshly baked treats permeates the air as patrons step into the cozy ambiance of Mai Bakery. From pillowy-soft Shokupan to sweet anpan, each creation reflects Micah's dedication to authenticity and quality. The bakery's commitment to using the finest ingredients ensures that every bite is a taste of culinary excellence. Whether you're a seasoned fan of Japanese baked goods or a newcomer eager to explore the world of unique flavors, Mai Bakery in Springville, Utah, promises a delightful experience that captures the essence of Japanese baking traditions.
